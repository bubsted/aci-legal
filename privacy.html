1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Agricultural Construction, Inc. ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with our internal point-of-sale application ("Application"). This Application is used exclusively for internal business operations and is not available to the general public.

2. Information We Access

The Application connects to QuickBooks Online via Intuit's API to access the following business data for internal operational purposes:

  • Product and inventory information
  • Sales invoice data
  • QuickBooks account credentials (handled entirely through Intuit's secure OAuth process)

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or disclose any business or customer data accessed through this Application to any third parties. All data remains within Agricultural Construction, Inc.'s internal systems and QuickBooks Online.

5. Data Storage

The Application runs on internal company hardware. QuickBooks data is accessed in real time via Intuit's API and is not stored independently by this Application beyond temporary session use.

6. QuickBooks / Intuit

Authentication with QuickBooks Online is handled through Intuit's OAuth 2.0 system. We do not store your QuickBooks username or password. Intuit's own Privacy Policy governs how Intuit handles your data. You can review it at intuit.com/privacy/statement.

7. Security

Access to the Application is restricted to authorized employees of Agricultural Construction, Inc. We use HTTPS for all communications between the Application and QuickBooks Online.
